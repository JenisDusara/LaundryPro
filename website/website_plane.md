`# LaundryPro — Website Plan

## Product
LaundryPro — laundry shop management software for Indian laundry and dry-cleaning businesses.

## Website Ka Goal
Shop owners ko convince karna ke demo try karen ya WhatsApp pe contact karen.

---

## Website Me Kya Chahiye

### 1. Navbar
- Logo aur "LaundryPro" naam
- Links: How It Works, Features, Reviews
- "Try Demo" button

### 2. Hero Section
- Bada headline: "Manage Your Shop. Grow Profitably."
- Ek Hindi line: "Register pe likhna band karo."
- Short description
- 2 buttons: "Try Live Demo" aur "WhatsApp Us"
- 3 stats: 500+ shops, 4.9/5 rating, 1 day setup
- App ka screenshot/mockup

### 3. Trust Bar
- Indian cities ke naam jahan use hota hai
- Ahmedabad, Mumbai, Delhi, Surat, Jaipur, Pune, etc.

### 4. Problem Section
- 3 problems jo shop owners face karte hain
  1. Orders notebook mein kho jaate hain
  2. Pata nahi chalta kitna revenue hua
  3. Labour tracking bahut mushkil hai

### 5. How It Works (4 Steps)
1. Customer search karo
2. Garments add karo
3. Bill generate hoga
4. Delivery mark karo

### 6. Features (7 Main Features)
1. **Customer Management** — naam, phone, address, search
2. **Billing & Orders** — 10 second mein bill, auto total, WhatsApp share
3. **Delivery Management** — saari pending deliveries ek screen pe, ek click mein mark delivered
4. **Reports & Analytics** — daily/monthly revenue report, kitna kamaya pata chale
5. **Labour Management** — worker ka kaam track karo, wages auto calculate, advance record
6. **Expense / Accounting** — rent, salary, bijli, raw material ka kharcha category wise track karo
7. **Service & Pricing** — apne garment ke rates khud set karo (shirt ₹30, suit ₹120, etc.)

### 7. WhatsApp Integration Section
- Customer ko automatic WhatsApp message bhejna
- Bill create hone pe message
- Delivery ready hone pe reminder
- Professional look — customer impressed hota hai

### 8. Testimonials (3 Reviews)
- Real shop owners ke quotes
- Rajesh Sharma — Ahmedabad
- Priya Patel — Surat
- Mohammed Raza — Mumbai

### 9. CTA (Call to Action)
- "Ready to run your shop the right way?"
- WhatsApp Us button
- Try Demo button

### 10. Footer
- Logo, copyright
- Try Demo aur WhatsApp links
